Features

Everything you need
to stay in sync

Built for the way real groups coordinate — simple, visual, and delightfully fast.

Shared groups

Create groups for your family, friends or team. Everyone sees each other's events the moment they're added.

Calendar sync

Sync your iOS calendar automatically. Work, sports, appointments — everything flows into one view.

Proposals & voting

Suggest an event and let the group decide. Perfect for dinners, trips, and weekend plans.

Notifications

Get notified about new events, votes on your proposals, and when members join your group.

Personal avatars

Choose a photo or emoji avatar so the group always knows who's behind each event at a glance.

6 beautiful themes

Sand, Midnight, Snow, Dusk, Forest and Rose — choose the palette that feels like home.

Calendar

Your week at a glance

Switch between month, week and day views. See color-coded events from every group member — so you always know who's doing what.

  • Month, week and day views
  • Color-coded per member
  • Filter by group
Groups

Bring everyone together

Create a group, share an invitation code, and you're set. Each member gets their own color so the calendar stays clear and readable.

  • Share with a simple invite code
  • See all members and their colors
  • Private and secure by default
Themes

Make it yours

Choose from six handcrafted themes. From warm sand to deep midnight — every detail has been considered so the app feels uniquely yours.

  • 6 themes: Sand, Midnight, Snow, Dusk, Forest, Rose
  • Dark and light options
  • Instantly applied everywhere
